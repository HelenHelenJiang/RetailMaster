enum NclDisconnectionReason
Enumerator
NCL_DISCONNECTION_LOCAL 

Disconnected by us (the NEA).

NCL_DISCONNECTION_TIMEOUT 

Nymi took too long to do something, so NCL disconnected it.

NCL_DISCONNECTION_FAILURE 

The Nymi behaved unexpectedly, so NCL disconnected it.

NCL_DISCONNECTION_REMOTE 

Disconnected by the Nymi.

NCL_DISCONNECTION_CONNECTION_TIMEOUT 

Disconnected due to no information requests.

NCL_DISCONNECTION_LL_RESPONSE_TIMEOUT 

Disconnected due to link-layer procedure timeout.

NCL_DISCONNECTION_OTHER 

Disconnected for an unknown reason.
